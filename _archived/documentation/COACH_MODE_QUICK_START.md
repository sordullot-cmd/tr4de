## 🎯 What Coach Mode Detects

### 1. **Revenge Trading** 🚨
Trades within 5 mins after a loss = PANIC MODE DETECTED

**Example:**
```
❌ Trade 5: -$500 loss at 10:15
→ 2 minutes later...
✓ Trade 6: +$3 revenge trade at 10:17 (still emotional)
```
**Impact:** Compounding losses through revenge

---

### 2. **FOMO Behavior** 📈
Increasing size after wins = GREED DETECTED

**Example:**
```
✓ Trade 3: +$200 win with 2 contracts
→ Confidence boost...
✓ Trade 4: Same strategy but 4 contracts (2x!)
```
**Impact:** Disproportionate risk after emotional high

---

### 3. **Loss Chains** 📉
3+ consecutive losses = STRATEGY BREAKDOWN DETECTED

**Example:**
```
Trade 1: -$100
Trade 2: -$150
Trade 3: -$200
Trade 4: -$175
= $625 lost in chain
```
**Impact:** Stop-losses too loose OR strategy adapted to wrong market

---

### 4. **Over-Leverage** ⚖️
Position size varies 300%+ = DISCIPLINE MISSING

**Example:**
```
Trade 1: 1 contract
Trade 2: 3 contracts
Trade 3: 5 contracts (after losses)
Trade 4: 2 contracts
```
**Impact:** Inconsistent risk management = account blowup risk

---

### 5. **AutoLiq Liquidations** 🔴
Account forcibly closed = CRITICAL FAILURE

**Why it happened:**
1. You exceeded broker's max loss threshold
2. Your positions were too aggressive
3. You had no daily stop-loss

**Impact:** Capital completely wiped out

---

### 6. **R:R Optimization** 💰
Current RR ratio vs optimal 1:2

**Example:**
```
Your Strategy:
- Avg Win: $300
- Avg Loss: $500
- RR: 0.60 (TERRIBLE - you risk 2x what you gain)

With 1:2 RR:
- Avg Win: $600 (2x risk)
- Avg Loss: $300 (1x risk)
- Potential improvement: +$8,500 (+185%)
```
**Impact:** Mathematical optimization of exits
